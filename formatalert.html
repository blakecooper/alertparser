Convert UB Facilities notices to UBIT Alerts

UBIT is responsible for posting UBIT Alerts when informed by UB Facilities that power outages will disrupt network and telephone service. Use this utility to convert the copied text from a UB Facilities notice (typically a PDF attached to an email) into a standard UBIT Alert format.
